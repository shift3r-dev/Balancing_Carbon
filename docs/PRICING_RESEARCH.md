# Pricing Research

## Decision

Balancing Carbon is positioned for Indian manufacturers, so the Phase 3 catalog uses INR:

| Plan | Monthly | Yearly | Position |
| --- | ---: | ---: | --- |
| Starter | INR 4,999 | INR 49,990 | Small facility or early carbon programme |
| Professional | INR 14,999 | INR 149,990 | Growing operations with compliance workflows |
| Enterprise | INR 49,999 | INR 499,990 | Complex reporting, governance, and support needs |

The yearly price is approximately 17% below twelve monthly payments. Enterprise+ is archived, not deleted, so old records retain referential integrity.

## Market Signals

- Greenio publicly lists INR 2,999/month for Growth, INR 5,999/month for Pro, and INR 49,999/month for Enterprise. It targets Indian BRSR and carbon-accounting buyers.
- CarbonLens lists annual Starter and Growth packages at INR 249,000 and INR 499,000, with Enterprise quoted separately. This validates an annual enterprise band near INR 500,000 for a more comprehensive offering.
- CarbonAnalytics lists a transparent global entry tier at GBP 69.99 per business unit per month and a Pro tier at GBP 299 per business unit per month, so the selected INR catalog remains approachable for the local market while leaving room for a contract-led enterprise sale.
- Large global vendors such as Persefoni use a free self-service offer plus a demo-led advanced tier, reinforcing that complex enterprise deployments are usually sold with a consultative motion.
